Welcome to OpenSTP

We welcome you on the page of OpenSTP. A open source sport trainings application. This application should help sport coachs or Physio Therapists to prepare the training and get feedback from the athlete. The Software is now under development. You can get the latest news on this site or on the youtube channel of Raphael Bircher. You can find the source of the program on GitHub. The project is under Apache License v 2.0.
